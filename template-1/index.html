About the council

Advocacy, connection, and support for gifted learners

The council is a nonprofit organization that brings together a diverse community of members from around the world. Our network includes classroom teachers, school leaders, university faculty, researchers, psychologists, parents, and others dedicated to gifted and talented children.

Through conferences, publications, and ongoing collaborations, the council highlights the needs of advanced learners, promotes research-based practices, and supports policies that ensure access to appropriate educational opportunities.
