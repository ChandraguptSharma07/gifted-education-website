Who we are

Warm, inclusive spaces for gifted learners

The Global Gifted Network brings together people who care deeply about gifted children and young people. Members include classroom teachers, enrichment coordinators, school leaders, researchers, and families.

Together, we share practical ideas, celebrate student strengths, and design learning experiences that are both challenging and joyful.
