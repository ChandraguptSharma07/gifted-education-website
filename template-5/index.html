Learning pathways

Design a route that fits each learner

This section is built for three core pathways. Edit the text and labels to match the structure of your own gifted or advanced learning program.

Pathway 01

Deep dive seminars

Short, intensive seminars that invite students to explore one concept in depth – from number theory to narrative design.

  • Weekly discussion sessions
  • Guided reading and mini-research
  • Capstone share-back

Pathway 02

Studio projects

Longer projects where students design, test, iterate, and present something that matters to them and their community.

  • Design briefs & constraints
  • Mentor check-ins
  • Public exhibition or sharing

Pathway 03

Independent inquiry

Self-directed investigations with coaching on planning, reflection, and next steps beyond the program.

  • Personal inquiry plan
  • Portfolio artefacts
  • Transition recommendations
