About Gifted Education CIC

Gifted Education CIC is a non-profit organization dedicated to identifying and nurturing gifted children from all backgrounds. We believe that every gifted child deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential, and we are committed to providing the resources and support they need to thrive.

Our mission is to create a world where giftedness is understood, valued, and nurtured. We work with educators, parents, and policymakers to advocate for the needs of gifted children and to ensure that they have access to high-quality educational opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

The term "gifted" generally refers to students who demonstrate high achievement capability in areas such as intellectual, creative, artistic, or leadership capacity, or in specific academic fields. These students require services and activities not ordinarily provided by the school to fully develop their capabilities.

Common characteristics can include: learning faster, reading early with great comprehension, a long attention span, understanding abstractions earlier, being curious and asking complex questions, and being bored with routine tasks.

While some gifted children may succeed without specialized support, many will not reach their full potential. Gifted education ensures that these students receive the appropriate level of challenge and intellectual stimulation necessary for their mental health and intellectual development.

NEP 2020 on Gifted Education

The National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 places a significant emphasis on identifying and nurturing gifted students. Key recommendations include:

  • Identification and Nurturing: Identifying gifted students and providing them with opportunities to pursue their strong interests and capacities beyond the general school curriculum.
  • Enrichment and Guidance: Providing supplementary enrichment material, guidance, and encouragement to students with unique talents.
  • Clubs and Circles: Establishing and supporting topic-centered and project-based clubs and circles at various levels.
  • Olympiads and Competitions: Conducting Olympiads and competitions across the country to ensure widespread participation.
